ProteinCHAOS — Planned Improvements

Here's everything we're going to implement. Click any item to mark your priority.

⚡ Performance (Physics Engine)

1

Cell-list WCA repulsion

Replace O(N²) loop with O(N) grid-based neighbor search. Enables repulsion for all system sizes (currently disabled above 1500 atoms). Expect 5–20× speedup for large proteins.

2

Precompute rotation matrix once per frame

Currently recalculated per-bond in the accumulation loop. Precomputing saves ~30% of accumulateChaos CPU time for dense structures.

3

Pre-baked Gaussian noise cache

Replace per-step Box-Muller with a 65536-entry Float32 ring buffer. Reduces random generation overhead by ~3×.

4

Ping-pong transferable buffers

Stop copying 33MB/frame. Use two Float32Arrays and transfer ownership back and forth. Near-zero memory allocation per frame.

🎨 Visual Effects & Rendering

5

ACES filmic tone mapping

Replace log-compression with ACES curve. Produces richer highlight roll-off and deeper shadow detail — the look of film photography vs a webcam.

6

Enhanced depth cueing + atmospheric fog

Use Z-depth for both brightness falloff AND color shift (cool→warm as atoms approach camera). Creates strong spatial depth illusion without 3D rendering.

7

2D canvas glow / bloom pass

Apply a fast CSS blur + compositing bloom effect on hot pixels. Gives the "long-exposure neon light" look that researchers love for cover art.

8

Auto-rotation / slow tumble mode

A toggle that slowly rotates the art-space Y-axis over time, continuously accumulating trails. Creates stunning corkscrew "time-lapse" renders.

9

Proper film grain (value noise)

Replace sin-hash grain with a 256×256 tiled value-noise texture. Smoother, more organic grain that responds to brightness (brighter areas get less grain).

🧬 Chain Coloring & Color System

10

Per-chain color pickers (dynamic)

Auto-detect chains on load, render a color swatch per chain in the sidebar. Click to open a native color picker. Works in both art-canvas and 3D view simultaneously.

11

New curated palettes + colorblind-safe set

Add "Publication" (Black/white-friendly), "Vivid" (high-saturation cover art), "Wong" (colorblind-safe 8-color), and "Pastel" (soft presentation colors).

12

Residue-property coloring

Color by residue type: hydrophobic (orange), polar (cyan), charged+ (blue), charged− (red), glycan (gold). Great for highlighting active sites.
